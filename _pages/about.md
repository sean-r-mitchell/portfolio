I am an Electrical Engineer with a focus on dynamical systems, machine learning, and robotics. Over the past decade, I’ve contributed to UxS solutions, intelligent systems, and sensor-integrated platforms. My work spans robotics, communications, GNC/PNT and autonomous systems. I have also led cross-functional teams, implemented modular software architectures, developed automated testing frameworks, and maintained CI/CD pipelines to ensure reliable and field-ready systems.

In addition to my industry experience, I am a life-long learner. I have a strong academic background in electrical engineering and remain actively involved with Academia. Some of my research interests include dynamical systems, chaos theory, information theory, machine learning, reservoir computing, recurrent neural networks, neuron state initialization, diffusion models, adversarial networks, automatic robotic systems, ML GNC/PNT, signal processing, software defined radio, additive manufacturing, and software architecture.

I am always open to connecting with fellow professionals and enthusiasts to share insights, collaborate on innovative projects, and explore new opportunities.
