An autonomous, self-guided parafoil to assist in recovery of high altitude balloon payloads under the UAH Space Hardware Club. Electrical subteam lead and software contributor. Designed custom PCB, simplified power supply, derived analytic vehicle model, and implemented 3DOF flight simulation with PIDF controller, conducted flight tests with quadcopter drones
