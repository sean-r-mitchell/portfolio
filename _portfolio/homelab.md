I run a network of home servers that provide a variety of self-hosted and open-source services for personal use. I have deployed a suite of automated tools that manage and serve multimedia formats. I also host a variety of web based services, through several hosts and docker images. These servers provide network security, monitoring, and management. Remote access and LAN bridging is performed via VPN. I maintain a local GitLab instance, including pipelines, on this network. This network also includes remote sensors that link in smart gardens, smart home devices, and home security systems
